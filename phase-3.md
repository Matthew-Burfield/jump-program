# Phase 3: Absolute Strength (Weeks 13–16)

**Goal:** Peak strength. Heaviest month. Unusually high set counts are deliberate — this is where the strength base gets cemented. Accessories prime the eccentric work coming in Phase 4.
**Stimulus:** Supramaximal clean pulls, near-max squats. Low reps, maximum intent.
**Tempo:** Standard controlled. The load is the stimulus — no strict tempo count.
**Frequency:** 3 lifting days (Mon/Wed/Fri) + 1 jump session (Sun)
**Deload:** Week 16 (Week 4 of this phase)

---

## Sunday — Jump Session

**Total window: 40 minutes**

**Low-effort warm-up (25 min):**
Pogos, skips, bounds, submaximal CMJs (60–70%), approach run-throughs, submaximal approach jumps
at 75–80%.

**Max effort (15 min):**
~10–12 full approach jumps at 100% intent.

**Before the max effort block — CNS primer check:**
- 2 measured CMJ reps (My Jump Lab)
- If either reads >10% below recent best → skip the max effort block entirely, keep as warm-up only

**Max effort rest:** 60–90 seconds between attempts. If the pop drops off, stop early.

*Real jump progress starts here. The strength base from the lifting is beginning to express itself.*

---

## Lifting Days (Mon / Wed / Fri)

### CNS Primers
*Always first, before any lifting.*

**Standing CMJ × 2 reps — measured via My Jump Lab**
- Max intent every rep, full reset between jumps
- 3 min full rest between jumps
- **If either jump reads >10% below recent best → cut all working sets by 50% today**

---

### Clean Pull — Supramaximal
*Heavier than your power clean max. Drive through the floor, full extension at the top — no catch.
This builds first-pull strength and teaches an explosive second pull. Power cleans will feel light
after this phase.*

| Week        | Sets × Reps | Load                |
|-------------|-------------|---------------------|
| 13          | 6 × 3       | 115kg (~105% clean) |
| 14          | 7 × 3       | 117.5kg (~107%)     |
| 15          | 8 × 3       | 120kg (~109%)       |
| 16 (Deload) | 4 × 3       | 105kg               |

---

### Back Squat — Near Max
*10-set weeks are not a typo. This is the absolute strength peak — rest fully between sets (3+ min).
Every set must be a quality set.*

| Week        | Sets × Reps | Load                |
|-------------|-------------|---------------------|
| 13          | 8 × 3       | 137.5kg (~83%)      |
| 14          | 10 × 3      | 142.5kg (~86%)      |
| 15          | 10 × 2      | 150kg (~91%)        |
| 16 (Deload) | 5 × 2       | 130kg               |

---

### Nordic Hamstring Curl — Eccentric Primer for Phase 4
*Full effort on the descent, controlled. These prime Phase 4's rack eccentric work — treat them
seriously, they are not junk volume.*

| Week        | Sets × Reps | Notes                           |
|-------------|-------------|---------------------------------|
| 13          | 3 × 5       | Heavy — RPE 9                   |
| 14          | 3 × 5       | Maintain load or progress       |
| 15          | 3 × 5       | RPE 9                           |
| 16 (Deload) | 2 × 5       | RPE 7                           |

---

### 2-Up / 1-Down Calf Raise — Eccentric Primer for Phase 4
*Use both legs to raise, lower on one under full control. Full ROM — heel below step at the bottom.
The slow single-leg eccentric is the stimulus.*

| Week        | Sets × Reps (each leg) | Notes                    |
|-------------|------------------------|--------------------------|
| 13          | 3 × 6                  | Bodyweight or light load |
| 14          | 3 × 6                  | Add load if comfortable  |
| 15          | 3 × 6                  | RPE 8–9                  |
| 16 (Deload) | 2 × 6                  | Reduce load              |

---

## General Days (Tue / Thu)

**Jump circuits in sand:** 20–30 sec per circuit, variety of jumps (toe touch jumps, tuck jumps,
single-leg hops, spin jumps). Rest fully between circuits. Sub-maximal only — not approach jumps.

**Tempo runs:** 30–45 sec @ controlled effort, **3 min rest** between. Increase reps/sets vs Phase 2.
Stay sub-lactic — uncomfortable but never painful legs.

No heavy lower body gym work on these days.

---

## Warm-Up (Every Session)

**Copenhagen planks: 3 × 20–30s each side** — non-negotiable, every session.

---

## Key Reminders

- **10 sets on the squat is intentional** — rest 3+ min between sets, don't rush
- **Clean pulls are supramaximal** — heavier than your clean max, load the bar accordingly
- **Accessories prime Phase 4** — Nordics and calf eccentrics build the base for rack eccentrics
- **Week 16 deload is mandatory** — adaptation from 8–10 set weeks consolidates here
