Notes from John Evans

Macro-cycle (6 month period)

Month 1: Volume building
Reps: 8-12
Sets: 2-5
Intensity 65-70% (depending on rep range)
Deload on the 4th week
General work

- For 2-foot jumpers: things like: sumo deadlifts and unilateral work
- For 1-foot jumpers: things like: deep squats and full range of motion
  3 days per week
  Start with a power exercise at 5x4 (high volume which we can intensify next phase)
  Squats in the 4-5 set range with 6-12 rep range
  Accessories in the 8-12 rep range

Phase 2
More specific work
5-7 reps in the 3-5 rep range
increase sets per week

- Two foot jumpers: things like:
  - clean pulls @ 90% intensity
  - power cleans @ 75-85% intensity
  - sometimes snatch pull @ 90% intensity

Follow the power work with max strength work (6-8 rep range)
Acceessories are slightly more specific (to jumping);

- Helps to prime the next cycle
- Week over week lower the reps and up the intensity to 80-85%
- Examples: RDL, calf raises

general work (tuesdays/thursdays)
"keep things a little bit more specific here, because it functions to increase the specificity on the general days, without letting the specific days get too specific." - this means we can add some small specificity on tuesdays and thursdays, so that monday/wednesday/friday doesn't need to be too specific (mimic jumping)
don't get too specific because that's how you get hurt
tempo running

Phase 1 + 2 = 1 month

Phase 3 (strength phase) (3 weeks)

- 10 sets of 3, or 10 sets of 2 force back squatting
- 105 - 110% of max power clean

Again, accessories potentiate the next cycle: in this cycle that means ecentric strength which are primers for the main lifts in phase 4

- nordic hamstring curl
- 2 up one down calf raise

general work
jump circits (toe touch, tuck, single leg hops) for 30-40 sec
tempo work 30-40sec runs with 3 min rest

Phase 4 (strength speed)

- Move barbells fast
  Power clean 2x per week and clean pull day and 3rd day

ecentric movements
Split squats, squats (110 - 115% of max)

partial ranges of motion

accessories: high velocity calf raises, high velocity RDLs (2 sets of 5-6 reps - low because of neural fatigue)

general work (get more general): body weight work, no jump circits, lower tempo work.

- The main work is getting more specific, so reduce the specificity on the general days (flip flop from phases 1-3)
