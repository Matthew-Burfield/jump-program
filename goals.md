# Phase 1 & 2 Success Metrics

### 1. Primary Physical Goals

- **Vertical Jump:** Increase standing vertical by **2+ inches** by the end of Week 16 (end of Month 3).
- **Back Squat:** Achieve a 5-rep max at **1.5x bodyweight** with perfect 4-3-1-0 tempo.
- **Relative Strength:** Maintain or slightly reduce body fat % while increasing bar speed.

### 2. Structural/Health Goals

- **Tendon Health:** Zero "sharp" patellar pain during or after pogo jumps.
- **Ankle Stiffness:** Noticeable reduction in "heel collapse" during depth landings.
- **Triple Extension:** Improved synchronization of hips, knees, and ankles during CNS primers.

### 3. Long-term "Moonshot" (Cycle 6)

- **The 35-inch Club:** Hitting a 35" max approach vertical.
- **Elite Strength:** 200kg Back Squat / 140kg Power Clean.
