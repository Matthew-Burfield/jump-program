| Weight | First session | Consolidated? | Sessions to consolidate | Issues |

| 80kg | Mar 14 | **No** | 7+ sessions and counting | Consistent RPE10 last sets; repeated rep failures |

### Problems identified

**1. Weight jumps too large**
The note on Jan 24 said "increase to 72kg" — then the next session went to 75kg (+5kg). Every weight jump has produced rep failures and a consolidation phase of several sessions. Standard micro-loading increments (1.25kg) would smooth this out significantly.

**2. Increases triggered too early**
The rule is: increase when last set is RPE 8 across 2+ consecutive sessions. In practice, increases have been triggered after a single session at RPE 8. The jump to 78kg happened after one RPE8 session at 75kg (Feb 03). That single session wasn't enough — 75kg wasn't fully owned yet.

**3. Currently stuck at 80kg**
80kg has been the working weight for 7 sessions since Mar 14. Last set has been RPE 9-10 every session, with rep failures on Mar 14, Mar 24, Mar 26, Apr 02. This weight is not consolidated. Do not increase.

### Recommendation

- **Stay at 80kg** until the last set is RPE 8 or below across 2 consecutive sessions
- **Next increase: 81.25kg** (add 1.25kg, not 2.5kg)
- Apply the 2-session rule strictly before every future increase
- The pattern suggests you can consolidate any given weight in 3-5 sessions when the jump is small — larger jumps cost you more sessions overall, not fewer
