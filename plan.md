# 8-Week Vertical Macrocycle: Phase 1 & 2

## Phase 1: Accumulation (Weeks 1–4)

**Goal:** Structural Integrity & Tendon Armor

- **Primary Stimulus:** Eccentric and Isometric loading.
- **Lifting Intensity:** 65-80% 1RM.
- **Jump Volume:** Low (Primers only).
- **Week 4:** Mandatory Deload (50% Volume).

## Phase 2: Transmutation (Weeks 5–7)

**Goal:** Power Conversion & Rate of Force Development (RFD)

- **Primary Stimulus:** Explosive Concentrics & Plyometrics.
- **Lifting Intensity:** 80-90% 1RM + Dynamic Effort (Light/Fast).
- **Jump Volume:** Moderate (Introduction of Depth Jumps/Bounds).

## Phase 3: Realization (Week 8)

**Goal:** Peak Expression & Testing

- **Primary Stimulus:** Specificity.
- **Lifting Intensity:** Very Low (60–70% 1RM, 1 main lift per session, maintenance only).
- **Jump Volume:** High Intensity / Low Volume — 3–5 max-effort approach jumps per session, full rest between each.
- **Sessions:** 3 (e.g. Mon/Wed/Fri). No new stimulus. No DOMS going into the test.
- **End of Week 8:** Max Vertical Test (Saturday).
